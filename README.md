# Task 4 - Dashboard Design

## Overview

This project was completed as part of a Data Analyst Internship Task focused on designing an interactive business dashboard.

The dashboard was built using Microsoft Power BI and the Superstore dataset. The goal was to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights through KPIs, visualizations, filters, and storytelling.

---

## Objective

* Create an interactive dashboard for business stakeholders.
* Track key business metrics using KPI cards.
* Analyze sales and profit performance.
* Identify trends, top-performing products, and customer insights.
* Present findings in a clear and visually appealing manner.

---

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Power BI
* Superstore Dataset

---

## Dataset Information

The Superstore dataset contains information about:

* Orders
* Sales
* Profit
* Customers
* Regions
* Categories
* Sub-Categories
* Products
* Customer Segments

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Dashboard

Key Performance Indicators:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Profit Margin (%)

Visualizations:

* Monthly Sales Trend
* Sales by Region
* Profit by Category

Interactive Filters:

* Region
* Category
* Segment

---

### 2. Product Analysis

Visualizations:

* Top 10 Products by Sales
* Top 10 Products by Profit
* Sales by Sub-Category
* Profit by Sub-Category

Purpose:

* Identify best-selling products.
* Analyze profitability across products and categories.

---

### 3. Customer Insights

Visualizations:

* Sales by Segment
* Profit by Segment
* Sales by State
* Orders by Region

Purpose:

* Understand customer behavior.
* Compare performance across segments and locations.

---

## Key Insights

* Technology category generated the highest profit.
* Consumer segment contributed the largest share of sales.
* Certain products generated high sales but low profitability.
* Sales performance varied significantly across regions.

---

## Features

* Interactive KPI Cards
* Dynamic Filters (Slicers)
* Trend Analysis
* Product Performance Analysis
* Customer Segmentation Analysis
